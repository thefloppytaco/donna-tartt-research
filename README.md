## What this is

Two tiers, by design:

- **Tier A — works *by* Tartt:** novels, short stories, essays, forewords/afterwords, introductions,
  contributed pieces, translations, audiobooks she narrated, and her on-record interviews.
  Catalogued in **[`Donna_Tartt_Collectors_Reference.md`](Donna_Tartt_Collectors_Reference.md)**.
- **Tier B — works *about* Tartt:** magazine profiles, signed posters, cover-blurb mentions.
  Catalogued in **[`About_Donna_Tartt_Items.md`](About_Donna_Tartt_Items.md)**.

Every factual claim links to its evidence — a saved web page, a scanned PDF, an auction record, or a
transcript — stored under [`assets/`](assets/) so the research survives link rot.

## Sourcing & rights

Most claims link to evidence saved under `assets/`. **One category is held privately:** full-book
scans of the works Tartt *contributed* to (only her specific contribution is relevant, and it's
already extracted into [`assets/sources/archive/excerpts/`](assets/sources/archive/excerpts/)). Those
full books live in a separate private archive and are cited here by publication / ISBN. Everything
else — press captures, dealer and auction records, foreign-edition pages, magazine covers — is here.

Material is reproduced for non-commercial research; rights to the underlying works remain with their
owners. See **[`LICENSE`](LICENSE)** — the original research and compilation is CC BY-NC 4.0;
third-party source captures stay under their owners' copyright. To request removal of anything,
open an issue.

## Accuracy & limitations

Claims are cross-checked against primary sources where possible. Documented corrections, and any
items that remain single-sourced or unsettled, are recorded in
[`reports/Verification_Report.md`](reports/Verification_Report.md). Per-edition caveats and points of
issue are noted in the relevant sections of the reference.
