About this Item

1992 Alfred A Knopf hardcover stated First Edition, first printing. The book is unread and in very good condition. There is some rubbing to the acetate dustjacket which has been price-clipped else good condition. This is a scarce variant printing of the first edition. The book and jacket are about 0.5cm shorter than the more common printing and the boards are glossy instead of matt with a 1.5cm black border extending around the cover image.

About this title

Synopsis

A transfer student from a small town in California, Richard Papen is determined to affect the ways of his Hampden College peers, and he begins his intense studies under the tutelage of eccentric Julian Morrow. BOMC & QPB Alt. Tour.

About the Author

DONNA TARTT is the author of the novels The Secret History, The Little Friend, and The Goldfinch. Her work has been published in forty languages and her third novel, The Goldfinch, was awarded the Carnegie Medal and the Pulitzer Prize for Fiction.
