First Edition Points and Criteria for The Secret History

Year Published: 1992
Author Last Name: Tartt
Author First Name: Donna
Publisher: Alfred A. Knopf
Original Price: $23.00
Pages: 524

The first edition of this nice read was published by Alfred A. Knopf in 1992. It was 524 pages long, and the original retail price was $23.00. The first edition can be identified by the following criteria: FIRST EDITION is stated on the copyright page with no statements of subsequent printings. Dust Jacket is transparent with price of $23.00 and three review blurbs by John Grisham, Jay McInerney, and Ruth Rendell.

We have run across two possible variations of the first edition. Both books in this photo are first printings. The one on the left has a yellow tone, and the one on the right has a gray tone. We have also observed the yellow tone on second printings, and based on this we believe that the gray tone preceeds the yellow tone.



Other first edition points for books by Donna Tartt include: The Goldfinch, Het puttertje.
